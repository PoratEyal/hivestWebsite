Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your NetVest account and associated personal data at any time.

Once you confirm, your profile, content, and personal data will be removed within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.

How to Delete

  • In the app: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete account.
  • By request: Email us at support@netvest.app using the email linked to your account. We may ask you to verify your identity.

Need Help?

If you have questions before deleting your account, please contact us at support@netvest.app.
